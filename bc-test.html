Musical Guest

Opening Night Concert

Leeland

Sunday, August 9th at 7:00 PM

Leeland to Open Bible Conference with a Night of Worship

Join us for a meaningful opening night as Leeland leads worship to begin this year’s Bible Conference. Based in Texas, Leeland is led by singer, songwriter, and multi-instrumentalist Leeland Mooring, alongside guitarist and co-writer Casey Moore. Together, they have become one of the most recognizable voices in modern worship music.

Since emerging in the mid-2000s, Leeland has released multiple albums and received four GRAMMY® nominations and eight GMA Dove Award® nominations. Their music is deeply shaped by the local church, with songs often born out of shared worship experiences rather than performance-driven settings.

Mooring is also a co-writer of the widely sung worship anthem “Lion and the Lamb,” a song embraced by congregations around the world. Known for crafting worship that invites participation, Leeland’s opening night concert sets a reflective and unifying tone for the weekend ahead.

74th Anniversary of the USA Southern Bible Conference

Radical Faith: Not By Might

From August 9–15, 2026, officers, soldiers, families, and friends will gather for a Spirit-led week of renewal and spiritual development. This year’s gathering calls us to a deeper, more radical faith—shaped not by our own strength, but by God’s presence and power at work among us. Delegates can look forward to heartfelt devotions, powerful corporate worship, and enriching Bible study sessions designed to renew hope, deepen trust, and strengthen their walk with God.

The week will also feature a dynamic Youth Conference, equipping the next generation to lead with faith, courage, and reliance on the Spirit, alongside engaging programs created for young adults. Special guest announcements and additional details are coming soon—stay connected as we prepare for all God will do.
