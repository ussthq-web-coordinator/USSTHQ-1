Introducing

Click above to control the video

Soundcast will be leading a project AT COMMISSIONING to record conversations with Salvationists from across the Southern Territory. The heart behind this is simple —We want you to sit down with someone who has been an inspiration to you and your walk with God. A parent? Grandparent? Corps Officer? Teacher? How did they help shape your faith and lead you to where you are today? Soundcast will preserve these stories to inspire and encourage others, creating an archive that reflects how God is moving through His people in the Southern Territory

Young Adults

This Is Your Moment.

At Commissioning 2025, you’re invited to be part of a powerful weekend of worship, connection, and purpose—with Saturday featuring special experiences for young adults. From community outreach and shared meals to passionate worship and send-offs for this year’s Summer Service Corps teams, it’s a day that puts your generation in the spotlight.

While the whole weekend brings us together, Saturday is a time to connect with others in your season of life, ask big questions, and lean into what God might be saying to you right now.

Pastors, mentors, leaders—encourage the young adults in your circles to show up.
This isn’t a side track or a youth event. It’s a meaningful invitation to step fully into the life of the Church and the mission we share.

Don’t sit this one out.
Come be part of something bigger at Commissioning 2025.

Youth Conference

At Commissioning 2025, the Youth Conference offers an exciting, Christ-centered experience tailored for children ages 5–12, with loving and secure childcare available for little ones aged 6 months to 4 years. While you engage in powerful worship and teaching, your kids will be growing in their own faith through age-appropriate activities, dynamic Bible lessons, music, and new friendships in a safe, nurturing environment.

Parents and pastors—this is more than childcare. It’s an investment in the next generation of disciples.

Spots are limited! Pre-registration and completed paperwork are REQUIRED by Friday, May 30th—no exceptions.

Don’t miss this opportunity for your children to be part of something unforgettable.
